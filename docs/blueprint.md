# **App Name**: Wildfire Resilient Hub

## Core Features:

- Emotional Visual Timeline: A compelling landing page featuring a hero image or visual sequence illustrating the stages of fire risk (mitigation, during-fire, post-fire recovery) to create an immediate emotional connection and convey urgency and resilience.
- Localized Resource Gateway: An intuitive user interface allowing selection of geographic context or general risk stage to dynamically display relevant external resources and support links, including organizations like Firewise.org and NET PBEM for mitigation.
- Pre-Fire Mitigation Toolkit: A dedicated section with actionable links and information for prevention and preparedness, detailing steps like property hardening and creating defensible space, addressing risks such as lack of rain, extreme weather, and brush.
- During-Fire Aid Directory: Provides critical emergency support resources for a live fire event, featuring direct links to organizations offering temporary housing, food assistance (Red Cross), and insurance aid (FEMA).
- Post-Fire Recovery & Resilience Hub: Offers comprehensive links and guidance for managing the aftermath of a fire, including resources for flood and mudslide mitigation, structural resilience, and long-term community support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A grounded and reassuring deep blue (#2D6186) to convey trust, stability, and calm amidst potential crisis.
- Background color: A very light, subtle blue-grey (#F0F3F4) to ensure readability and maintain a clean, supportive feel.
- Accent color: A vibrant teal (#3CDDCE) used for calls to action and important highlights, symbolizing renewal and positive action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for its blend of modern readability and humanist warmth, suitable for delivering critical information clearly and empathetically.
- Clear, modern, and symbolic icons for navigation and resource categories, enhancing quick comprehension of crucial information and support types.
- A clean, responsive grid-based layout for clear content hierarchy, with large, impactful images prominently displayed to foster emotional connection and highlight key information.
- Subtle hover effects on clickable elements and gentle page transitions to improve user interaction and guide focus without distracting from the serious nature of the content.
